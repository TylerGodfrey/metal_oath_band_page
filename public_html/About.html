About Us

Metal Oath has been around for five years now. Hank founded the band and was solo for the first year Metal Oath was in existence. He found a drummer after one year in fellow member, Anthony. They played together for two years before they found the other members, Bernard and Radford. They now play together and make great music.

Metal Oath have released one album so far, and are currently working on their second album. Hank is hoping that after the release of The Last True Warrior, Metal Oath will begin a world tour.
