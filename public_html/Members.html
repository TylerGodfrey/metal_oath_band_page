Members of Metal Oath

  • Hank
    • Hank has been the lead singer of Metal Oath for the last five years.
    • Hank has played in other bands before he joined Metal Oath. He previously was apart of New Day, Tree Whispers, and The Best Time.
    • He has at least 7 different tattoos on his right arm
    • Hank can play any kind of guitar.

  • Radford
    • Radford is the lead guitarist and has been for two years now.
    • Radford has been known to write his own songs during his free time. He often trys to persuade Hank to sing them.
    • When not playing or writing songs, Radford can often be found hiking with his dog, Demon.

  • Bernard
    • Bernard plays the bass guitar for Metal Oath. He has been in that position also for two years.
    • Bernard has taken the lead guitar whenever Radford gets sick and he does a great job.
    • Secretly, Bernard can't sing very well. Thankfully, the others tend to drown him out.
    • At home, Bernard has a pet fish named Jerry, and a cat named Tom.

  • Anthony
    • Anthony is the guy in the back who bangs on the drums. He has been the drummer for four years now.
    • Anthony lives in an apartment building where he likes to practice his drumming. Needless to say, he has been warned many times about his noise.
    • He is the only member of the group not to have any tattoos, as he is afraid of needles.
    • In high school, Anthony played percussion in his school's concert band.
